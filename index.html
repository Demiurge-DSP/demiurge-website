Recently my (now former) domain provider, freeavailabledomains.com which handles usa.cc second level domain names, had a rash of sites hosting malware from its service. This has lead to some antvirus and antimalware vendors such as Malwarebytes, automatically issuing a warning to the user and a report to the service provider for any usa.cc domain loaded into the browser, even with no actual malware involved.

I was unaware of this and had been having an issue with my server's firewall configuration, and asked a friend to see if my site loaded for him. This resulted in his antivirus program reporting my site. My site that at the time had only static html, that can't possibly serve up any malware! My site was blocked by freeavailabledomains, and when I contacted them about it they had this to say: "We do not allow malware. Please use other service."

Nobody even looked. As a result I no longer have any usa.cc domains
